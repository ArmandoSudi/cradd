Values

The CRADD Non-Profit Organization’s mission is to work for sustainable development of both Democratic Republic of Congo and our continent by the research,
training and actions in its areas of intervention.

Moral Probity and Intellectual Integrity

Our successes and our policies and guiding principles reflect the high level of moral and intellectual probity we seek in all actions and relationships with third parties. It is characterized by integrity, honesty, righteousness and rationalism. Following the rules of ethics and science to realize our vision, to do our duty.

Professionalism

Members of the Center for Sustainable Development (CRADD) are characterized by the quality of the work of someone with experience and expertise. Thus the ability to ensure a commitment to society and meet its expectations.

Mutual love

The priority of our actions is to serve our fellow men for the development of all man and each man.
